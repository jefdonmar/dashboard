Deliver all types of campaign emails through one reliable platform

Streamline is an email application that allows for users to always deliver the best content to subscribers, they way that their subscribers want their content.

Upon receiving your log-in Credentials, you will be able to Add Subscribers to your email list services based on the content that they want to see. Further segmentation is done by tagging the content and also ensuring that individual subscribers are getting what they want, when they want.
